### Veranda Blend® Clover Vertica™
Starbucks Veranda Blend® Clover Vertica™ is a great choice for those who enjoy the strong flavor of espresso but prefer a smoother, less concentrated taste. The drink is made by adding hot water to a shot of espresso, creating a delicious and satisfying beverage.

It contains only 15 calories, with virtually no fat, cholesterol, or sugars.The beverage also contains 225 mg of caffeine, making it a great option for a morning kickstart or an afternoon pick-me-up.The coffee’s quality is highlighted by the simplicity of its ingredients, which consist only of water and espresso. A Tall Starbucks Veranda Blend® Clover Vertica™ typically starts at around $2.95, but prices may vary by location.
